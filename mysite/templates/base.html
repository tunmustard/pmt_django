FIND OUT MORE ABOUT OPPORTUNITIES TO WORK WITH US IN YOUR REGION.

Primetals Technologies is dedicated to engineering and manufacturing excellence in the metals industry. A joint venture of Mitsubishi-Hitachi Metals Machinery and Siemens, the innovative and technological strengths of both companies have been merged to form a globally operating enterprise that will be an elite supplier of state-of-the-art technologies, plants, products and services for the iron, steel and nonferrous industrial sectors.

From complex engineering projects to effective business management, we are a company that thrives on technology where innovation in every area is valued. As the world leader in metallurgical plant solutions, we work passionately to continuously improve and challenge ourselves to accomplish new goals, increase our customers’ competitiveness, and make a lasting contribution to society. It is our people who help us achieve this vision and whilst doing this, they enjoy diverse careers across our global operations with opportunities to develop and unfold their creative potential.
